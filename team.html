Our Team

Founder

Chanelle Chow is currently a student at The University of British Columbia completing her degree in Biology. She has a passion for teaching, Art, and Science. She is an assistant instructor of Chinese Brush Painting classes at Shadbolt Centre for the Arts and has also been a tutor in Math and Science for over three years. She continues painting and has been studying traditional Chinese Brush Painting for over 13 years.

Coordinator

Amelia Chiu graduated from the faculty of Education at The University of British Columbia and has worked in the B.C. School system as a teacher for 12 years. She has over 20 years of tutoring experience and has worked with a variety of students of all ages from kindergarten to Grade 12.

Our Instructors

Our instructors are specialized in various subjects that are offered, including Mathematics, English, and Sciences. Qualified tutors are selected through a practical assessment by a B.C. qualified instructor, and they include a variety of postgraduate students, professional tutors, and instructors. To ensure the best experience, we put our best effort into matching clients with a suitable instructor from our team.

We carefully screen and hire instructors based on their high standard of knowledge on the subjects offered, teaching ability, and experience. Sometimes, even if a person is an expert on a subject, he may not have the ability to communicate it in a clear and understandable way. This is why we ensure that our instructors have both the knowledge and capability to educate.
