Programs

We provide amazing programs

School Semester Programs

We offer supplementary Private Tutoring, Preview Programs, Review Lessons, and Exam Preparation for students who are needing extra help in school or are wanting to get ahead. We also offer Private Fine Arts Lessons in Drawing and Painting. Our skill range covers Elementary to Post Secondary subjects. Read more below or contact us for a consultation of which program is right for you. Small group lessons are available for each program at a group rate.

Private TutoringPersonal academic assistance

Tutoring services are offered during the school year and are meant to help students understand classroom material, keep up with learning, or accelerate beyond the classroom. Our tutoring is individualized to the students’ needs after identifying their areas of concern and a program is created to maximize their achievement. Our tutoring is flexible and a schedule is made between the tutor and client. Tutors meet at the convenience of our clients, often at our clients’ homes or nearby libraries.

PreviewGet ahead of the class

Preview is a fast paced program that will go over the curriculum of a course that the student has not yet learned in school. The program is meant to give background knowledge on a subject in order to help the student become familiar with and apply the concepts during the school year.

Exam PreparationTake the exam with confidence

Our Exam Preparation Program helps students achieve their goals on a big test or final exam. We go over previous exams with students and help answer any questions that they have about the material. Our instructors will assess their weak points, offer exam and study advice, and provide practice material based on the curriculum.

ReviewMastering the fundamentals

Review is the best way to solidify material that has been learned. Our review program helps students who have previously struggled in a course or need a refresher before advancing to the next grade level.

Private Fine Arts LessonsEngage your creative side

Lessons are offered in Visual Arts including Drawing and Sketching, Watercolour Painting, and Chinese Brush Painting. Music Lessons are also offered. Contact us for more details.
